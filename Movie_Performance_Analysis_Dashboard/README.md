# Movie Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes global movie performance data to evaluate box office revenue, production budgets, profitability, and return on investment (ROI) across genres, directors, ratings, and time periods.

The dashboard provides insights into what drives commercial success in the film industry and supports data-driven decision-making for studios, producers, and investors.

---

## Objective
- Analyze box office performance trends over time
- Compare profitability and ROI across movie genres
- Identify top-performing directors by box office revenue and profit
- Examine the relationship between movie ratings, budget size, and financial performance
- Support strategic investment and production decisions in the film industry

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Key Insights
- Total box office revenue significantly exceeds total production budget, resulting in strong overall profitability.
- Action, Science Fiction, Animation, and Adventure genres generate the highest box office revenues.
- A small group of directors account for a disproportionate share of total box office and profit.
- Movies with moderate to high budgets tend to achieve stronger box office performance, but ROI varies widely.
- Certain age ratings consistently outperform others in terms of revenue generation.
- Box office performance shows strong long-term growth, with noticeable acceleration in recent decades.

---

## Recommendations
- Prioritize investment in high-performing genres such as Action, Science Fiction, and Animation.
- Leverage proven directors with strong historical profitability for high-budget productions.
- Balance production budgets carefully to maximize ROI rather than focusing solely on box office size.
- Align movie ratings with target audience size to optimize commercial outcomes.
- Use historical performance trends to inform long-term production and release strategies.
